Computer Engineering

This field, a branch of Electrical Engineering, combines Software Engineering and Computer Science with Electrical Engineering. Computer engineering allows careers within the worlds of software and hardware.

Common subfields and branches include:

- Artificial Intelligence

- Computer Networks

- Computer Systems

- Cybersecurity

- Embedded Systems

- Hardware Engineering

- Software Engineering

Computer Engineers are employed in industry, government, education, and consulting firms. They are in high demand and have average salaries of around $100,000. Their work can be found in avionics, viehcle control systems, mobile applications, biomedical devices, and defense systems.
