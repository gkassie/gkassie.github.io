My Major: Computer Systems Engineering

This major is a hybrid of Software Engineering/Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. Computer Systems Engineering allows careers within the worlds of Software and Hardware.

Common subfields and branches include:

- Artificial Intelligence

- Computer Architecture

- Computer Networks

- Cybersecurity

- Embedded Systems

Computer Systems Engineers are employed in industry, government, education, and consulting firms. They are in high demand and have average salaries of around $100,000.

I enjoy STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) and hands-on project-based classes. I plan to study CSE at ASU's Ira A. Fulton Schools of Engineering and be part of Barrett, the Honors College.
